# 📊 Blinkit Sales & Operations Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Blinkit’s sales and operational performance using *Power BI* to understand customer purchasing trends, category performance, and business KPIs.

The dashboard was developed as a self-learning business analytics project to strengthen practical understanding of *data visualization, KPI tracking, and business decision-making* using retail sales data.

*Project Duration:* December 2025
*Project Type:* Self-Learning Business Analytics Project
*Tools Used:* Power BI, Microsoft Excel, Power Query, DAX

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance and revenue trends
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Evaluate customer purchasing behavior
- Track operational KPIs and sales insights
- Understand product demand patterns for business decision-making

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- *Total Sales Revenue*
- *Total Orders*
- *Average Sales Value*
- *Average Product Ratings*
- *Category-wise Sales Performance*
- *Outlet Size & Location Analysis*
- *Item Type Performance*
- *Fat Content Analysis*
- *Sales Trend Insights*

---

## 🔍 Business Insights Generated

### 1. Category Performance Analysis
Identified product categories contributing the highest sales and customer demand.

### 2. Customer Purchase Patterns
Analyzed customer buying behavior based on product preferences and sales contribution.

### 3. Outlet Performance
Compared sales performance across outlet sizes and locations to understand revenue distribution.

### 4. Product Demand Trends
Evaluated high-performing item categories to identify demand concentration.

### 5. Business Decision Support
Generated insights that can support pricing, inventory planning, and category optimization.
